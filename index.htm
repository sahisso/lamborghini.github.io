LAMBORGHINI URUS

The ULTIMATE SUV and the ITALIAN MASTERPIECE

The 'LAMBORGHINI URUS' is a SUV manufactured by Italian automobile manufacturer LAMBORGHINI. It was unveiled on 4 December 2017 and was put on the market for the 2018 model year. The name comes from the urus, the ancestor of modern domestic cattle, also known as the aurochs.
